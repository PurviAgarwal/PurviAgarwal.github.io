Hybrid Electric Vehicle Kits

The pioneering technology of regenerative braking enables the Hybridtronics HEV kit to convert vehicle stopping energy into electric charge. The kit utilizes the energy of the moving vehicle as it is decelerated or brought to stop by the driver, stores it in the smart battery box, and uses this energy to accelerate the vehicle next time.
