Imagine the future of transportation - imagine a cleaner, smarter and more integrated system of mobility. Now imagine if this shared vision could be brought to life in an efficient, cost effective manner. This is the vision that we, at iX, aspire to achieve every day.

iX Energy is a technology company based in New Delhi, India, building intelligent electric transport solutions for India and the world. We aim to be the leading global player in the cleaner and smarter future of connected, autonomous, shared and electric mobility.

We’re on a mission to disrupt India’s freight and public transport and make it smarter through our Semi and Full Electric Trucks and Buses. We offer an intelligent low-cost option to convert existing Diesel/CNG vehicles into Hybrid and full Electric vehicles.

We’re looking to partner with Governments and Corporates who share our vision to transform sustainable mobility in India.
